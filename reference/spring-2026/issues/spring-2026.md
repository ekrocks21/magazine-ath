# Return.

The second issue of The Local Lookout. The ospreys come back. A mariner in
1609 writes down the first description of these Highlands. A Quaker pays
thirteen shillings for Sandy Hook in 1680. A ferry at 7:42 every morning
crosses the most important piece of water in American history. A humpback
washes ashore at North Beach on the first of April. And the coast, as
always, listens.
