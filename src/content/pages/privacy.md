# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 29, 2026*

The Local Lookout is published by Above the Hook, operated by Eric and Mary
from Highlands, New Jersey. This policy explains what information we collect,
why we collect it, and how we protect it.

## What we collect

**Account and subscription data.** When you subscribe or sign in, we collect
your email address. If you purchase a print subscription or single issue, we
also collect the shipping name and address you provide at checkout. Payment
processing is handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your credit
card number.

**Session data.** We use a short-lived session cookie (`magazine_session`) to
keep you signed in. It contains no personally identifiable information beyond
a reference to your account, and it expires after 30 days of inactivity.

**Analytics.** We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which measures page visits
without tracking individuals, setting cookies, or fingerprinting browsers.
We see aggregate numbers — how many people read a given article, which pages
are popular — not individual browsing histories.

**Emails we send.** When you request a sign-in link, we send a single
transactional email via Resend. When you subscribe, we send a confirmation
email. We do not send marketing email without your explicit consent.

## What we don't collect

We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not use advertising networks. We do
not embed third-party trackers. We do not build behavioral profiles.

## How we use your information

- **Email address** — to send your magic sign-in link, your subscription
  confirmation, and, if you opt in, occasional editorial dispatches.
- **Shipping address** — to mail your print copies. Stored only as long as
  needed to fulfill active subscriptions.
- **Session cookie** — to let you stay signed in between visits.
- **Aggregate analytics** — to understand which parts of the magazine people
  read and to improve the site.

## Data retention

- **Email and entitlement records** — kept as long as your subscription is
  active, and for one year after it lapses, in case you resubscribe.
- **Session tokens** — expire after 30 days; invalidated immediately on
  sign-out.
- **Print order records** — kept for seven years for tax and fulfillment
  purposes.

## Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

- Tell you what data we hold about you.
- Correct an error in your email or shipping address.
- Delete your account and all associated data.

Write to [shop@abovethehook.com](mailto:shop@abovethehook.com) and we'll
respond within five business days.

## Children

The Local Lookout is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect information from anyone under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If we make a material change, we'll update the date at the top and send a
brief note to active subscribers. Continued use of the site after the updated
policy is posted constitutes acceptance.
